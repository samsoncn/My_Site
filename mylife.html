About Me

I was born and raised in Hong Kong, and I moved to Canada when I was fourteen. My name is Samson and I am from Hong Kong. I am currently living in Canada. I am an undergraduate student at the University of Toronto, pursuing a Bachelor of Art and Science in Statistics and Linguistics. I have developed my interest in programming during high school. Although I am not studying computer science in univeristy, I learn programming and do side-project on my own regularly. I have enhanced my natural ability and skills in programming and data analysis through every opportunity that I got involved.

I successfully built my own PC as my first hardware project after graduating high school. I also worked at Animoca Brands and OliveX in Hong Kong on a software engineer intern position in the past two summers. It was my pleasure to work on this position and develop my communication, problem-solving and researching skills. I am a diligent worker, and passionate about my work. I continue to seek the opportunity to grow and further my connections and the development of my computing and social skills set with dynamic summer positions.
